Built by students,
for new traders.

TradeWise was born from a simple frustration — too many new traders were stepping into the world of investing without any real guidance, making the same avoidable mistakes over and over again.

At its core, TradeWise is an intelligent algorithm that watches how you trade and invest. It doesn't just track your numbers — it understands your patterns. When it spots a recurring mistake, whether that's panic-selling, over-concentrating in one sector, or mistiming entries, it doesn't lecture you. It teaches you.

Based on what it finds, TradeWise surfaces curated educational videos, explains the specific concept you're struggling with, and gives you actionable context directly tied to what just happened in your portfolio. No generic tutorials. No information overload. Just the right lesson at the right moment.

And we're just getting started — TradeWise will soon recommend specific tools your brokerage already offers, bridging the gap between what platforms provide and what users actually use.
